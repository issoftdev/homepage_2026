Our History

The history of ISSOFT’s growth, innovation, and partnership with clients.

History Timeline

2020. 04

Relocation & Expansion to IS Building

Relocated and expanded corporate headquarters to the 5th floor of IS Building, Dong-gu, Daegu.

2017. 07

ISO 9001:2015 Certification

Acquired ISO 9001:2015 Quality Management System Certification to standardize operations.

2016. 06

Smart Factory Verified Supplier Pool Registered

Officially registered as a verified smart factory deployment supplier under the Ministry of SMEs and Startups (MSS).

Relocation to Daegu Knowledge Service Center

R&D Department Certification

Venture Business Certification

L-CRIS Copyright Registration
