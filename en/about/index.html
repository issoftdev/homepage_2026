About Us

Your trusted IT partner for industrial success, ISSOFT.

CEO Greetings

ISSOFT is an enterprise software company that has grown with a strong focus on smart factory deployments, particularly MES/POP systems. Since our founding in 2013, we have accumulated extensive field experience in factory automation and enterprise systems integration (SI). Since 2016, we have been officially registered as a certified supplier for the Ministry of SMEs and Startups (MSS) Smart Factory program, driving digital transformation across Korean manufacturing plants.

While we build high-performance management systems that stably process large-scale production data via our proprietary L-CRIS framework, we also design and build custom web-based solutions tailored to each company's unique needs beyond the factory floor. Recently, the growing share of these web and mobile-based business solutions has emerged as another key growth engine for ISSOFT.

Every member of ISSOFT is dedicated to thoroughly understanding our clients' requirements,
serving as a partner that builds trust beyond technology. Thank you for your continued support.
